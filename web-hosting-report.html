Web Hosting Report

Questions:

What is a web server? Why would you need one?

A web server is a computer that stores and delivers web content to users. It's what makes internet users capable of accessing the web.

Explain why getting a domain name is not enough to put your site up on the web.

Although you have the name to attach to your website, you still need a place for your website to be stored and accessed from. It's kind of like having the address without the house.

What service does a web hosting company provide?

Web hosting companies provide you with access to their web servers so that internet users can access your site.

What are the most important characteristics to look for in selecting a good web hosting company?

Speed, Affordability, Bandwidth, and Reliability.

On average, what is the price range for hosting a website?

Around 6 to 9 dollars per month.

What are the most important features for you when reviewing a hosting plan?

As a college student, affordability is a big factor for me, but speed and reliability is also important.
